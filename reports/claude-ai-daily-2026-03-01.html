Be More Literal With Claude 4.x Models

Claude 4.x models (including 4.5 and 4.6) now prioritize precise instructions over "helpful guessing." If you ask for a dashboard without specifying charts and filters, you may get a blank frame. Power users are reporting better results by spelling out every desired element explicitly, rather than relying on Claude to infer what "above and beyond" looks like. Treat prompts more like contracts.

Ask Claude to Ask You Questions First
